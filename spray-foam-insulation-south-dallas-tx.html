Spray foam options for South Dallas TX

  • Open-cell spray foam – commonly used in attics and rooflines for sound and air sealing.
  • Closed-cell spray foam – denser foam with a higher R-value per inch, often used in walls, crawlspaces, and garages.

The best choice depends on your structure, attic design, and budget. A good installer will explain where each foam type works best.
